Last Updated: April 2025

1. General Information

This website is a personal portfolio created by Moritz Philipp Haaf. Protecting your personal data is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle and protect your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Collection & Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage statistics (e.g., page views, session duration, device and browser type). GA4 uses cookies to measure how visitors interact with the site.

The analytics cookies are non-essential and only loaded after you give consent via the cookie‑consent banner. You may decline or withdraw consent at any time without affecting your browsing experience.

3. Cookies

The following cookies may be set:

  • _ga: Distinguishes users. Duration: 2 years.
  • _gid: Distinguishes users. Duration: 24 hours.
  • _gat: Throttles request rate. Duration: 1 minute.

To manage your cookie preferences, click the “Manage Cookie Preferences” link in the footer or reload the page to view the banner again.

4. Contact via Email

If you contact me via email, your personal data (e.g., name, email address, message content) will only be used for the purpose of communication and will not be shared without your consent.

5. External Links

This site may include links to external services such as GitHub, LinkedIn, or Streamlit. I am not responsible for their data protection practices. Please refer to the respective providers’ privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

According to the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and restrict the use of your personal data. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde).
